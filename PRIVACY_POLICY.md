# Privacy Policy for Libri

**Last Updated: December 5, 2025**

The Blank State Team ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Libri mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide to Us

**Account Information**
When you create an account or sign in using Google Sign-In, we collect:
- Your name (as provided by Google)
- Your email address
- Profile picture URL (if available)

**User Preferences**
- Reading preferences (favorite genres, authors, languages)
- App settings and customizations
- Terms of service acceptance status

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Usage Data**
- App interaction data (features used, screens visited)
- Reading history and progress
- Search queries within the app
- Book downloads and favorites

**Device Information**
- Device type and model
- Operating system version
- Unique device identifiers
- App version

### 1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

**Open Library**
If you connect your Open Library account, we may access:
- Your Open Library username
- Borrowed books information
- Reading lists

**Project Gutenberg**
We do not collect personal information from Project Gutenberg. Book downloads are processed directly.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Account Management**: To create and manage your user account
- **Personalization**: To provide personalized book recommendations
- **App Functionality**: To enable features like reading lists, bookmarks, and sync across devices
- **Communication**: To send important updates about the App (you can opt out)
- **Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns and improve the App
- **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Storage

Your data is stored using:
- **Firebase Realtime Database**: For user preferences and reading data
- **Local Device Storage**: For downloaded books and cached content

### 3.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:
- Secure data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Firebase Security Rules
- Authentication through Google Sign-In
- Regular security updates

---

## 4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do **NOT** sell your personal information. We may share data in the following circumstances:

### 4.1 Service Providers
- **Google Firebase**: For authentication and database services
- **Google Play Services**: For app distribution and updates

### 4.2 Third-Party APIs
- **Open Library API**: Book metadata and borrowing (no personal data shared)
- **Gutendex API**: Book catalog and downloads (no personal data shared)

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by law or to:
- Comply with legal obligations
- Protect our rights or property
- Prevent fraud or security issues

---

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

### 5.1 Access and Control
You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Update or correct your information
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data

### 5.2 How to Exercise Your Rights
- **In-App**: Access Profile > Settings to modify preferences
- **Account Deletion**: Contact us at theblankstateteam@gmail.com
- **Data Export**: Contact us at theblankstateteam@gmail.com

### 5.3 Opt-Out Options
- Disconnect Open Library integration anytime
- Sign out to stop data collection
- Uninstall the app to remove local data

---

## 6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.
